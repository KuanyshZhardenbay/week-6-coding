What is the "SafeRoom"?

An extreme help site for helping people, who struggles with anxiety, panic attacks or/and hysterics at the moment. There are some tips and ways to deal with panic attacks, helpline for extremal situations, numbers of available therapists, resources to get distracted so people can faster and easier calm down and recover from the unforeseen situations.
